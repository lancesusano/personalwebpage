Comfort Room

           The comfort room is where great ideas come from. Often it's been said that influential figures conceptualize their crafts inside the bathroom. And this is a phenomena that does not require a scientific method to understand. I often produce profound ideas and insights as the water from the shower pours down my scalp. I truly owe the shower for the productive research topics and musical outputs that I've materialized throughout the years. Before engaging myself in various academic writings, I always see to it that I take a bath first; so that I can outline and organize my thoughts to yield an efficient outcome.
